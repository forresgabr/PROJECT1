CREATE A TOUR THAT MEETS YOUR REQUIREMENTS

Te Papa Tours allows you to experience the musuem without limitations. Select the exhibits you want to see while specifying any requirements... Are you traveling with kids? Will you need interactive designs, seating stations or wheelchair access?
Select what relates to you and we'll create the perfect visual tour, so you can navigate the musuem with no fuss.
Te Papa Tours is an easily accessible app that you can download prior to your visit via the Apple store or Google Play. Plan your trip in advance for fun with your family and friends.

HOW DOES IT WORK?

Te Papa Tours uses an algarithm that calculates the best route of your tour while taking into account your chosen exhibits and specified requirements.
As you're enjoying the exhibits your mobile phone will be signalling our beacons through bluetooth technology. This allows us to notify you with further information like the oportunity to scan QR codes and experience our displays with audio or simply where the nearest bathroom is. The option to talk to one of our friendly employees is easy to! With just one tap your location is sent to their paiger and within seconds you can ask them your burning questions.

WHO IS IT FOR?

Museums shouldn't be a luxury and should always be available to everyone. New Zealand is full of vibrant people and 24 percent of our population are identified as disabled, thats a total of 1.1million. Being New Zealand's most popular musuem we are visited by flocks of tourists where a fraction of these people are also disabled. Our goal at Te Papa is to welcome everyone through social inclusion and make it simple for everyone to experience Te Papa at it's fullest.
Through innovative technology we want to make your experience with us as comfortable as possible.

QR CODES

Te Papa Tours also has the option to scan QR codes so you make the most of the information we have to offer. Simply head to the home page and select scan, your camera will open and once scanned provide you with a audio guide and related artwork. While there are descriptions underneath the artworks QR can take you that extra step fruther.

ADD TO YOUR FAVOURITES

You can add more than the perfect tour to your favorites. How about that painting you saw on the third floor and the audio guide that went along with it? Or the kiwiana tea towels you're thinking of buying online? With the Favourites page you can save artworks and have a mini gallery in your pocket.
